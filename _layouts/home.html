About GTL/KG

Community

As property owners and managers with nearly 100 years’ experience in Southern California, we know how important it is for a business integrate itself in the community. We make it our mission to connect our tenants with neighborhood resources so that they become flourishing and active members of their new homes.

Neighborhood enhancements

We’re committed to making our cities the best they can be. We recruit top businesses in dining, shopping, beauty, entertainment, and more to improve the visitor and residential experiences in our neighborhoods.

Family operated

Since our start in 1923, GTL/KG has been completely family operated. Four generations have made sure that our tenants—ranging from Fortune 500 companies to start-ups—feel at home in our properties. Our tenants are part of the family!
