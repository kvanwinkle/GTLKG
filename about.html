About GTL/KG

GTL/KG Properties is a family-operated real estate holdings company, specializing in premiere properties across LA’s vibrant Westside. GTL/KG began in 1923, when Russian immigrant, William Katz, built his first property in East LA. Although he was cap maker by trade, Katz recognized the value of owning real estate in the ever-expanding Los Angeles market. Katz’s two children soon took over the management of the properties, and when his grandchildren joined the business in the late-1970s, the company began to flourish.

Today GTL/KG properties can be found in Beverly Hills, West Third Street, Melrose, and Beverly Grove.

  • Over 90% of our tenants have multiple locations.
  • Numerous locations are occupied by publically-traded companies.
  • We have an extensive screening process to make sure our tenants add a vibrancy to their individual neighborhoods
  • We help develop outreach programs
