Where better to develop than the hottest
spot in the city?

Our Beverly Grove property is directly across the street from LA’s Original Farmers’ Market and The Grove, LA’s #1 tourist destination. Originally an annex for the Farmers’ Market, the current structure was built in 1992 as a professional office/warehouse setting. After housing a wholesale hat business for more than 20 years, legendary architecture firm Gensler redesigned the building for the world’s first IMAX VR Centre. Head there to enjoy today’s most immersive technology – virtual reality. Whether you’re fighting Stormtroopers in Star Wars: Trials on Tattooine or diving into the 360-degree world of VR narrative, the centre is one of the city’s most dazzling entertainment venues.

The former warehouse space features ceilings that are two stories high. Upstairs, the building features large office suites and a conference room with dramatic views of the city. Indeed, this building remains a truly one-of-a-kind location for its combination of office and open space at the center of the city’s largest tourist region.

Come explore our Beverly Grove flagship and prepare to be entertained.
